Working and thriving

We’re proud to offer benefits that help you to feel your best and do your best.

Digital-first office

Slack is our headquarters, where we support a hybrid, fully distributed workforce around the globe. With remote roles, office-free onboarding and flexible working practices, we’re set up for ever-changing realities, responsibilities and schedules.

Just for you

Receive an annual allowance for professional development, together with a monthly stipend that you can use for anything. That means anything, from a gym membership or your pet sitter to a new work look – whatever matters most to you.

Health and wellness

Free medical, dental and vision care for you and your family; Slack pays 100% of your premiums. We also offer access to a range of free mental health and well-being resources.

Parental perks

A lot of us have families, of all shapes and sizes and types. We welcome each new addition with generous parental and new-child bonding leave, along with access to child-care assistance services for children up to the age of five.
